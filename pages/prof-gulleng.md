**Prof. Gulleng Daskyes Yohanna** is from Plateau State. An accomplished academic with specialized training in
Criminology and security studies.

He is a consultant and security analyst whose area of
Interest spans through legitimacy and policing, prison
Management, violence studies, private security,
Human rights and anti-corruption research.

He was consulted and is still consulting for local and
International organizations on security, human rights,
Early warning and early response.
